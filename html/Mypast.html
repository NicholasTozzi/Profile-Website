Career

From my senior year of high school, to about 3 years after high school I hadn't really thought much about my career

  • Publix Manager
  • Pyramid Scheme Aficionado

Pretty short list, right? I didn't have a high image of myself, so I set the bar pretty low for my career goals. It wasn't until my 2 year expirience working at a Chili's that I realized that I didn't want to be there forever. The work environment got so unprofessional, and toxic to where I told myself that I deserve better. Very odd upbringing story, but sometimes you just have to learn life lessons the hard way.

Leisure

Right after high school, I had found out that there was a local smash tournament near me. I had always dreamed of entering tournaments for this game since I played Super Smash Brothers Melee in 2001. Entering competitions taught me more about myself than I new was possible from playing a kids fighting game. Mentality, and the ability to self improve were the skills that I learned in Leisure, and now want to put that energy towards my career!
