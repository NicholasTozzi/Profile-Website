My Start at something new

My first name is Nicholas, but everyone calls me by my last name. I have gone to school for computer science, but I didn't really enjoy the languages I learned along the way. I remember as a kid loving editing my MySpace page, and using custom HTML. I mostly just copy and pasted various widgets from the internet, but my profile was special because it was something I had created. I realized my falling out with programming may not have been my fault, so I took matters into my own hands.

If 2020 has taught me anything, its that I can't be a server in Orlando Florida forever. COVID-19 has given me a mental vacation I didn't know I needed. The quarentine still hasn't ended, but I knew my vacation needed to. School felt unsettling to go back to, but I know I needed to make change in my life. I know it is going to be an uphill battle, but I'm looking forward to climbing to the top.
